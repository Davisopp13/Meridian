  - CtPipBar: passes onAwaitingCase + onResumeAwaitingCase to MinimizedStrip
- Decisions made (for Davis review):
  - handleTruePauseCase writes duration_s=c.elapsed so elapsed survives tab close while paused.
    The restoreCaseFromRow then uses duration_s directly for paused cases (no timestamp math).
    This avoids needing a new paused_since column. PRD said "no migration if text column" and
    did not mention a new column for paused state, so this is the minimal approach.

    prop keeps working without touching SwimlaneTray.jsx (which is out of PRD scope §7).
  - Mutual exclusion: both handlers clear the other flag before setting their own.
